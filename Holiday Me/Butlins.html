Bognor Regis

Bognor Regis is a small seaside town on the south coast of England in the county of West Sussex. It is situated approximately 70 miles south west of London. The town can trace its roots back to when it was a small fishing village. The town still has its fishermen, operating small inshore boats from the town's beaches, keeping alive the town's traditional link with the sea.

Minehead

Minehead Information Centre is located in the Beach Hotel opposite the West Somerset Railway near the Seafront. Available at the centre are maps, walking literature, local crafts and information on Minehead and the surrounding area.
